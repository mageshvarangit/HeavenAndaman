Best Time to Visit Andaman: Month-by-Month Guide (2024)

Planning a trip to Andaman? The best time depends on what you’re looking for! November to May is ideal for sightseeing and water sports, with clear skies and calm seas. June to September brings monsoon showers, making it perfect for budget travelers and nature lovers who enjoy lush greenery.

Top 10 Things to Do in Andaman Beyond Beaches

Andaman isn’t just about beautiful beaches! Go snorkeling or scuba diving at Havelock Island, trek through the lush Mount Harriet National Park, or explore the eerie beauty of the Limestone Caves in Baratang. Experience the magical bioluminescent waters at night, take a glass-bottom boat ride, or visit the historic Cellular Jail for a light and sound show.

Andaman Honeymoon Guide: Romantic Places & Must-Do Activities

Make your honeymoon unforgettable in Andaman! Walk hand in hand on the white sands of Radhanagar Beach, take a private sunset cruise, or enjoy a candlelight dinner by the shore. Visit Neil Island for its peaceful vibe, or indulge in a couple’s snorkeling experience in the crystal-clear waters.

How to Reach Andaman: Flight, Cruise & Ferry Options Explained

Reaching Andaman is easier than you think! Flights from major cities like Chennai, Kolkata, and Delhi land at Port Blair, the main entry point. For a scenic journey, opt for a cruise from Chennai, Kolkata, or Vizag, taking 3–4 days. Once in Andaman, hop between islands via government and private ferries for a smooth travel experience.
