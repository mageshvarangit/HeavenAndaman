Ertiga / Xylo (Similar)

The Ertiga and Xylo are stylish and fuel-efficient MUVs, ideal for small families and business travelers. With spacious interiors, comfortable seating, and powerful air conditioning, they ensure a smooth and enjoyable ride. Their reliable performance and ample luggage space make them a great choice for airport transfers and sightseeing. Whether cruising through city roads or exploring scenic landscapes, these vehicles provide a hassle-free travel experience.

Traveller (17-Seater)

The 17-seater Traveller is designed for group tours, corporate outings, and family trips, offering spacious and comfortable seating. Equipped with modern safety features, air-conditioning, and a powerful engine, it ensures a smooth ride even on hilly terrains. Its large windows provide a panoramic view, making it perfect for sightseeing adventures. With ample luggage storage and ergonomic seating, passengers can travel long distances without discomfort.

Traveller (24-Seater)

Ideal for large groups, the 24-seater Traveller offers a blend of safety, comfort, and reliability for long and short journeys alike. Its spacious interior, push-back seats, and air-conditioned environment make travel enjoyable, even on longer routes. Perfect for school trips, corporate travel, or large family vacations, this vehicle ensures ample legroom and luggage space. Designed for smooth performance, it can handle Andaman’s roads with ease.

Tata Winger (12-Seater)

The Tata Winger is a compact yet spacious 12-seater van, perfect for medium-sized groups looking for a comfortable journey. Known for its fuel efficiency and reliability, it is an excellent choice for city tours, airport transfers, and short excursions. Its plush seats, air-conditioning, and low-noise engine make every ride peaceful and relaxing. The vehicle’s smooth suspension ensures minimal bumps, offering a stress-free travel experience.
