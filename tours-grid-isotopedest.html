Cellular Jail – A historic monument that tells the story of India's freedom struggle, offering a moving light and sound show in the evening.

Ross Island – Once the British administrative capital, this island now features picturesque ruins overtaken by nature, giving it an eerie yet beautiful appeal.

Radhanagar Beach – Known as one of Asia’s best beaches, it boasts powdery white sand, turquoise waters, and breathtaking sunsets, perfect for a tranquil escape.

Wandoor Beach – A serene beach known for its coral reefs and gateway to the Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park, ideal for a peaceful day out.

Long Island – A secluded island with unspoiled beaches, dense forests, and rich marine life, offering an offbeat adventure for explorers.

Jolly Buoy Island – A no-plastic zone famous for its pristine coral reefs and crystal-clear waters, perfect for snorkeling and glass-bottom boat rides.
